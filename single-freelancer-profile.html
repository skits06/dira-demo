About Me

Abraham is CEO & Founder of Infers Group, a knowledge management company based in Gaborone. He is a Communication and Public Engagement specialist , having been in the field for 10 years and accrued in-depth techniques and experience in working with scientists, business community, communities, policy makers and the media. He is interested in working with African scientists and various stakeholders towards building science development and participation among Africans as well as creating research ecosystems in Africa. Abraham is also a recipient of the prestigious Wellcome International engagement fellowship which he aims to work on exploring best practices in incentivising science development process in Africa by bringing like minded people, scientists and communities together to explore trade opportunities in the science process. He is also currently leading a creative science engagement project initiative in Botswana, South Africa and India called Arting Health For Impact. An initiative that explores street art and biomedical science engagement.
